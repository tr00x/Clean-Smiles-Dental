How it connects · what you're paying for

Your API is read-only. Here's how each approach fixes that.

Today your OpenDental key can readRead = look at data only. Your current key can show schedules, patients and forms, but can't change anything. your data but can't writeWrite = create or change data, like booking an appointment or saving a filled-out form. This is what your read-only key can't do — yet.. Every approach below closes that gap. You choose; we recommend HybridRead with the official API, write to your own database. Full power, nothing extra to pay, all on your server..

Reads ✓ Your key already does this — show schedules, slots, patients, forms, documents.
Writes ✗ This is the gap — booking, saving forms, signatures, status changes. The four approaches below are the different ways we add it.

What this means for you: you pay me once to build the apps — you don't pay any monthly platform fee, and with the recommended Hybrid approach you don't pay OpenDental anything extra either. Reads use your existing free key; writes go to your own database on your own server. The paid OpenDental write-API (~$60/mo) stays optional — and since it still can't do signatures or payments, Hybrid is simply the better fit. If you ever want it, we switch any feature over without a rebuild.
